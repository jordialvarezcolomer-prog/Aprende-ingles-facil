# A2 - Ejercicios de Reading

## Tema 1: Email Informal

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el email y responde

**Email:**
```
Dear Maria,

How are you? I'm fine, thank you. Last weekend I went to the beach 
with my friends. We had a great time! The weather was beautiful and 
sunny. We swam in the sea and played volleyball. In the evening, we 
had dinner at a seafood restaurant. It was delicious!

When can we meet again?

Best regards,
John
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿Dónde fue John el fin de semana?
   - [ ] Mountains
   - [ ] Beach
   - [ ] City

2. ¿Qué actividades hizo?
   - [ ] Swimming and reading
   - [ ] Swimming and volleyball
   - [ ] Tennis and volleyball

**Respuestas:** 1. Beach | 2. Swimming and volleyball

## Tema 2: Anuncio de Trabajo

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el anuncio

**Anuncio:**
```
WANTED: English Teacher
Location: Madrid, Spain
Experience: 2+ years
Salary: €1,500-€2,000/month
Requirements: Native English speaker or C1 level

Apply by: December 31st, 2024
```

**Preguntas Verdadero/Falso:**
1. El trabajo es en Barcelona. _____ (V/F)
2. El salario es €1,500-€2,000 por mes. _____ (V/F)
3. Se requieren 3 años de experiencia. _____ (V/F)

**Respuestas:** 1. False | 2. True | 3. False

## Tema 3: Biografía Corta

### Ejercicio 1: Lee sobre una persona

**Texto:**
```
Tom is a 25-year-old musician from London. He started playing guitar 
at age 7. He has performed in many concerts and festivals. His dream 
is to record an album. He spends most of his time practicing and 
composing music. In his free time, he enjoys reading and traveling.
```

**Completa:**
1. Tom es de _____.
2. Empezó a tocar guitarra a los _____ años.
3. Su sueño es _____.

**Respuestas:** 1. London | 2. 7 | 3. record an album

## Tema 4: Horarios y Programas

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el horario

**Horario de la Escuela:**
```
Monday to Friday:
9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Math
10:30 AM - 10:45 AM: Break
10:45 AM - 12:00 PM: English
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch
1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Science
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿A qué hora es English? _____
2. ¿Cuánto dura el descanso? _____

**Respuestas:** 1. 10:45 AM - 12:00 PM | 2. 15 minutes
