# A2 - Ejercicios de Gramática

## Tema 1: Presente Simple

### Ejercicio 1: Completa con la forma correcta
1. I _____ coffee every morning. (drink / drinks)
2. She _____ at the office. (work / works)
3. They _____ football on Sundays. (play / plays)

**Respuestas:** 1. drink | 2. works | 3. play

### Ejercicio 2: Negativos
Forma negaciones:
1. I like pizza. → I _____ like pizza.
2. He works here. → He _____ work here.

**Respuestas:** 1. don't | 2. doesn't

---

## Tema 2: Pasado Simple (Regular Verbs)

### Ejercicio 1: Transforma al pasado
1. play → _____
2. study → _____
3. work → _____
4. want → _____

**Respuestas:** 1. played | 2. studied | 3. worked | 4. wanted

### Ejercicio 2: Completa las frases
1. I _____ a movie yesterday. (watch / watched)
2. They _____ at the beach. (play / played)

**Respuestas:** 1. watched | 2. played

---

## Tema 3: Presente Continuo

### Ejercicio 1: Forma el presente continuo
1. I / work → I _____ _____
2. She / read → She _____ _____
3. They / eat → They _____ _____

**Respuestas:** 1. am working | 2. is reading | 3. are eating

---

## Tema 4: Comparativos y Superlativos

### Ejercicio 1: Completa con comparativos
1. English is _____ (difficult / more difficult) than Spanish.
2. My house is _____ (big / bigger) than yours.

**Respuestas:** 1. more difficult | 2. bigger

### Ejercicio 2: Superlativos
1. This is the _____ (good / best) restaurant in the city.
2. She is the _____ (smart / smartest) student.

**Respuestas:** 1. best | 2. smartest
