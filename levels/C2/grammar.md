# C2 - Ejercicios de Gramática

## Tema 1: Estructuras Gramaticales Rarificadas

### Ejercicio 1: Fronting Extremo y Cleft Sentences
1. Only through rigorous adherence to methodological protocols _____ the validity of conclusions _____ ensured.
   - [ ] can / be
   - [ ] is / being
   - [ ] may / have been

2. It is precisely the _____ of conventional paradigms that marks the significance of this innovation.
   - [ ] deconstruction | [ ] subversion | [ ] challenging

**Respuestas:** 1. can be | 2. subversion

---

## Tema 2: Nominalización Avanzada

### Ejercicio 1: Transformaciones complejas
1. The fact that he had deliberated extensively → His _____ _____
2. Because the data were heterogeneous → The _____ of the data

**Respuestas:** 1. extensive deliberation | 2. heterogeneity

---

## Tema 3: Register y Style Variability

### Ejercicio 1: Reescribe en registro ultra-formal
Versión original: "The findings show that students learn better with break time."

Versión formal requerida: "The empirical evidence _____ that temporal intervals of respite _____ cognitive retention."

**Respuestas:** 1. substantiates | 2. potentiate

---

## Tema 4: Syntactic Ambiguity y Precision

### Ejercicio 1: Desambigua mediante estructura sintáctica
Oración ambigua: "The students read the material carefully."

¿Qué interpretación se sugiere reordenando?
1. Para énfasis en "las estudiantes": _____
2. Para énfasis en la minuciosidad: _____

**Respuestas:** 1. The students, not the faculty, read the material carefully | 2. The students read the material with considerable care
