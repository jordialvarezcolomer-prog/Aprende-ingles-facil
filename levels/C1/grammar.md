# C1 - Ejercicios de Gramática

## Tema 1: Estructuras Gramaticales Complejas

### Ejercicio 1: Fronting y Inversion Avanzada
1. Only by adopting innovative strategies _____ the company _____ its market position.
   - [ ] did / improve
   - [ ] can / improve
   - [ ] might / improve

2. Not only _____ she fluent in five languages, but she's also an accomplished musician.
   - [ ] is | [ ] was | [ ] has

**Respuestas:** 1. can improve | 2. is

---

## Tema 2: Nominalization and Abstract Nouns

### Ejercicio 1: Transforma verbos a sustantivos abstractos
1. To establish → _____
2. To accommodate → _____
3. To demonstrate → _____

**Respuestas:** 1. establishment | 2. accommodation | 3. demonstration

### Ejercicio 2: Completa con formas nominalizadas
1. The _____ of the project was unprecedented. (succeed)
2. His _____ to excellence earned him recognition. (commit)

**Respuestas:** 1. success | 2. commitment

---

## Tema 3: Advanced Conditionals

### Ejercicio 1: Conditionals with inversion
1. _____ I known about the opportunity, I would have applied.
   - [ ] Had | [ ] If | [ ] Should

2. _____ the government to implement strict measures, the crisis could be avoided.
   - [ ] Were | [ ] Should | [ ] Had

**Respuestas:** 1. Had | 2. Were

---

## Tema 4: Subjunctive and Formal Structures

### Ejercicio 1: Subjuntivo formal
1. It is imperative that every employee _____ the protocol.
   - [ ] follows | [ ] follow | [ ] has followed

2. The committee recommends that the proposal _____ reviewed.
   - [ ] be | [ ] is | [ ] has been

**Respuestas:** 1. follow | 2. be
