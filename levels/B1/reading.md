# B1 - Ejercicios de Reading

## Tema 1: Artículo de Noticia

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el artículo

**Artículo:**
```
TECHNOLOGY COMPANY OPENS NEW OFFICE IN MADRID
Yesterday, TechCorp announced the opening of its new headquarters 
in Madrid. The company plans to hire 500 employees in the next year. 
The new office will focus on software development and artificial 
intelligence research. "This is an exciting opportunity for Spain," 
said the CEO, "and we're committed to contributing to the tech industry."
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿Cuántos empleados va a contratar TechCorp?
   - [ ] 250
   - [ ] 500
   - [ ] 1000

2. ¿Cuál es el enfoque del nuevo lugar de trabajo?
   - [ ] Sales and marketing
   - [ ] Software and AI
   - [ ] Design and branding

**Respuestas:** 1. 500 | 2. Software and AI

## Tema 2: Ensayo Corto

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el ensayo sobre educación

**Ensayo:**
```
IMPORTANCE OF CONTINUOUS LEARNING
In today's world, continuous learning is essential for personal and 
professional growth. Technology changes rapidly, and new skills are 
required regularly. Whether through formal education, online courses, 
or self-study, investing in learning provides numerous benefits. 
It increases job opportunities, boosts confidence, and keeps the mind 
active. Therefore, lifelong learning should be a priority for everyone.
```

**Preguntas Verdadero/Falso:**
1. Continuous learning es importante solo para negocios. _____ (V/F)
2. El aprendizaje en línea es una opción válida. _____ (V/F)
3. El aprendizaje continuo aumenta oportunidades de trabajo. _____ (V/F)

**Respuestas:** 1. False | 2. True | 3. True

## Tema 3: Reseña de Libro

### Ejercicio 1: Lee la reseña

**Reseña:**
```
"THE ADVENTURE BEGINS" - Book Review
Author: Sarah Miller

This thrilling novel tells the story of a young explorer who discovers 
a mysterious island. The plot keeps you engaged throughout, with 
unexpected twists and turns. The characters are well-developed and 
relatable. However, the ending felt a bit rushed. Overall, it's an 
excellent read for adventure lovers. Rating: 4/5 stars.
```

**Completa:**
1. ¿Quién es la autora? _____
2. ¿Cuál es la calificación? _____
3. ¿Cuál es la crítica negativa? _____

**Respuestas:** 1. Sarah Miller | 2. 4/5 stars | 3. The ending felt rushed

## Tema 4: Guía Turística

### Ejercicio 1: Lee la guía sobre Barcelona

**Texto:**
```
BARCELONA: A CITY GUIDE
Barcelona is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast. 
Known for its unique architecture, especially the works of Antoni Gaudí, 
the city attracts millions of visitors yearly. Popular attractions 
include the Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, and Las Ramblas. The city 
offers excellent beaches, shopping, and dining. The best time to visit 
is spring or fall when the weather is pleasant.
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿En qué costa está Barcelona? _____
2. ¿Cuál es el arquitecto famoso? _____
3. ¿Cuál es la mejor época para visitar? _____

**Respuestas:** 1. Mediterranean coast | 2. Antoni Gaudí | 3. Spring or fall
