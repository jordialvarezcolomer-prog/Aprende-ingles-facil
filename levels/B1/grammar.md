# B1 - Ejercicios de Gramática

## Tema 1: Present Perfect

### Ejercicio 1: Completa con present perfect
1. I _____ (live) here for 5 years.
2. She _____ (visit) Paris three times.
3. They _____ (finish) their homework already.

**Respuestas:** 1. have lived | 2. has visited | 3. have finished

### Ejercicio 2: Preguntas
Forma preguntas:
1. _____ you _____ (see) that movie?
2. _____ he _____ (work) here long?

**Respuestas:** 1. Have you seen | 2. Has he worked

---

## Tema 2: Past Continuous

### Ejercicio 1: Transforma a past continuous
1. I / work / when he called → I _____ _____ when he called.
2. She / sleep / at 10 PM → She _____ _____ at 10 PM.

**Respuestas:** 1. was working | 2. was sleeping

### Ejercicio 2: Contrasta con Simple Past
1. While I was cooking, she _____ (arrive).
2. They _____ (watch) TV when the lights _____ (go out).

**Respuestas:** 1. arrived | 2. were watching / went

---

## Tema 3: Condicionales (Tipo 1 y 2)

### Ejercicio 1: Primer Condicional
1. If it rains, I _____ (stay) at home.
2. If she studies hard, she _____ (pass) the exam.

**Respuestas:** 1. will stay | 2. will pass

### Ejercicio 2: Segundo Condicional
1. If I _____ (be) rich, I would travel.
2. If she _____ (have) time, she would help us.

**Respuestas:** 1. were | 2. had

---

## Tema 4: Passive Voice

### Ejercicio 1: Transforma a voz pasiva
1. They build houses. → Houses _____ _____ by them.
2. She wrote the book. → The book _____ _____ by her.

**Respuestas:** 1. are built | 2. was written

### Ejercicio 2: Identifica voz pasiva
Mark V (voz pasiva) o A (voz activa):

1. "The cake was eaten by the children" _____
2. "John wrote a letter" _____

**Respuestas:** 1. V | 2. A
