# B2 - Ejercicios de Gramática

## Tema 1: Perfect Continuous Tenses

### Ejercicio 1: Present Perfect Continuous
1. I _____ (study) English for three years.
2. She _____ (work) on this project since morning.

**Respuestas:** 1. have been studying | 2. has been working

### Ejercicio 2: Past Perfect Continuous
1. We _____ (wait) for an hour before he arrived.
2. They _____ (live) there for 10 years before moving.

**Respuestas:** 1. had been waiting | 2. had been living

## Tema 2: Inversiones y Énfasis

### Ejercicio 1: Inverted Structures
1. Only after finishing the work _____ he leave.
   - [ ] did | [ ] will | [ ] would

2. So famous is this restaurant _____ you need reservations months ahead.
   - [ ] that | [ ] which | [ ] where

**Respuestas:** 1. did | 2. that

---

## Tema 3: Modal Verbs Avanzados

### Ejercicio 1: Modals para deducciones
1. She's not answering the phone. She _____ be in a meeting.
2. He couldn't have known. He _____ have been asleep.

**Respuestas:** 1. might/could | 2. must

### Ejercicio 2: Condicionales Mixtos
1. If I had studied harder, I _____ passed the exam.
2. If you focused, you _____ achieve your goals.

**Respuestas:** 1. would have | 2. could

---

## Tema 4: Subordinadas y Relative Clauses

### Ejercicio 1: Relative clauses
1. The book _____ I bought yesterday is excellent.
   - [ ] which | [ ] who | [ ] where

2. The woman _____ daughter is my friend is a doctor.
   - [ ] whose | [ ] whom | [ ] which

**Respuestas:** 1. which | 2. whose

### Ejercicio 2: Omisión de pronombres relativos
1. The person _____ you met yesterday called.
2. The film _____ we watched was great.

**Respuestas:** 1. (who/that can be omitted) | 2. (that/which can be omitted)
