## Tema 1: Artículo de Análisis

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el artículo sobre inteligencia artificial

**Artículo:**
```
THE FUTURE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming every aspect of our lives, 
from healthcare to finance. Machine learning algorithms can now diagnose 
diseases more accurately than human doctors. In finance, AI systems detect 
fraudulent transactions in seconds. However, this rapid advancement raises 
ethical concerns about job displacement and data privacy. Experts advocate 
for responsible AI development with proper regulations. The future depends 
on balancing innovation with ethical considerations.
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿En cuántos sectores se menciona la aplicación de IA?
   - [ ] 1
   - [ ] 2
   - [ ] 3

2. ¿Cuáles son las preocupaciones éticas mencionadas?
   - [ ] Solo privacidad
   - [ ] Desplazamiento laboral y privacidad
   - [ ] Solo desplazamiento laboral

**Respuestas:** 1. 2 (healthcare and finance) | 2. Job displacement and data privacy

## Tema 2: Crítica Literaria

### Ejercicio 1: Lee la crítica

**Crítica:**
```
REVIEW: "ECHOES OF THE PAST"
This contemporary novel explores themes of identity and memory through 
the journey of a woman returning to her hometown. The author's narrative 
technique is innovative, weaving past and present seamlessly. Character 
development is exceptional, yet the pacing occasionally falters in the 
middle chapters. The prose is lyrical, evoking strong emotions. Despite 
minor flaws, this is a compelling exploration of human resilience.
```

**Verdadero/Falso:**
1. El tema principal es viajes y aventura. _____ (V/F)
2. La técnica narrativa es tradicional. _____ (V/F)
3. El desarrollo de personajes es excepcional. _____ (V/F)

**Respuestas:** 1. False | 2. False | 3. True

## Tema 3: Ensayo Argumentativo

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el ensayo sobre sostenibilidad

**Ensayo:**
```
SUSTAINABLE BUSINESS: A NECESSITY, NOT AN OPTION
Companies today face mounting pressure to adopt sustainable practices. 
Climate change and resource depletion demand immediate action. 
Organizations like Patagonia and Tesla demonstrate that profitability 
and environmental responsibility coexist. Yet many corporations resist 
change, citing costs. However, long-term investments in sustainability 
yield financial returns while protecting our planet. The question is 
not whether businesses should go green, but how quickly they can adapt.
```

**Preguntas:**
1. ¿Qué argumento es el PRINCIPAL? 
   - [ ] Companies lose money going green
   - [ ] Sustainability is necessary for business survival
   - [ ] Patagonia invented sustainable business

2. ¿Cuáles son los ejemplos de empresas?
   - [ ] Apple and Microsoft
   - [ ] Patagonia and Tesla
   - [ ] Google and Amazon

**Respuestas:** 1. Sustainability is necessary for business survival | 2. Patagonia and Tesla

## Tema 4: Reporte Técnico

### Ejercicio 1: Lee el reporte de investigación

**Reporte:**
```
RESEARCH FINDINGS: REMOTE WORK PRODUCTIVITY
A two-year study of 5,000 employees revealed that remote workers were 
13% more productive than office workers. However, collaboration decreased 
by 8%. The study concluded that hybrid models optimize both productivity 
and team interaction. Recommendations include flexible schedules and 
regular virtual meetings to maintain company culture.
```

**Completa:**
1. Porcentaje de aumento de productividad: _____
2. Disminución en colaboración: _____
3. Modelo recomendado: _____

**Respuestas:** 1. 13% | 2. 8% | 3. Hybrid model
